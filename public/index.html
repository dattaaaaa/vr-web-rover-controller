Local Camera Stream to Meta Quest

Stream your mobile's camera to your Quest headset over your local Wi-Fi, using an IP Webcam app.

Mobile Setup (Enter IP Cam URL) Quest Viewer

How to Use:

  1. On your Mobile Phone:
    • Install an "IP Webcam" application (e.g., "IP Webcam" by Pavel Khlebovich on Android, or a similar app on iOS).
    • Start the IP Webcam server in the app.
    • Note the video stream URL it provides. This will be a local network URL, like http://192.168.1.10:8080/video (the exact path like /video or /shot.jpg or /live depends on your IP Webcam app).
    • Ensure your mobile phone and your Meta Quest headset are connected to the same Wi-Fi network.
  2. On the "Mobile Setup" Page:
    • Open the Mobile Setup page (you can do this on your computer or another device).
    • Enter the full video stream URL from your IP Webcam app and click "Set Stream URL".
  3. On your Meta Quest Headset:
    • Open the Quest Viewer page in the Meta Quest Browser.
    • Click "Connect for Stream URL & Controls". The video from your phone should appear.
    • Click "Enter VR" to see controller inputs displayed.

Important: The video stream is local. The Render server only helps set up the connection and relay controller data.
